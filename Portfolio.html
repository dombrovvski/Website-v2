About

Currently a senior at UPenn, I’m majoring in Visual Studies, with a concentration in Philosophy and the Science of Seeing. I also have a minor in Cognitive Science, focused on computation and cognition.
I’m a designer and aspiring front end web developer, so much of my inspiration comes from my experience with creating, studying, and admiring art. I’ve always appreciated the beauty in simplicity, and I try to bring that through in my work. Form and function should be harmoniously balanced, much like, say, a Gregorian chant.
My logo is highly influenced by my background in the Chinese language. I started studying Mandarin when I was 11, and eventually chose 黛秋玥 as my Chinese name in homage to Lin Daiyu in Dream of the Red Chamber. I have studied abroad in Shanghai twice, once during my sophomore year in high school, and again during the summer of my sophomore year in college.

Sella Redesign

Primary Tools: Sketch, HTML, CSS

Previous Next

Sella is a C2C resale startup that aims to revolutionize the way people engage with existing marketplaces. My job as a designer was to produce a process for item intake that would be utilized not only by paid workers, but also by volunteers for Sella’s associated nonprofit, Mighty Good Things. It was also my responsibility to revitalize the color palette and to create an overarching app design.
